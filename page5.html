"A  logoless company is a faceless man."
-David Airey

As I was finishing through my website content, I look for quotes that would be relevant to the page and came across with this one from David Airey:


"A logo doesn't need to say what a company does. Restaurant logos don't need to show food, dentist logos don't need to show teeth, furniture store logos don't need to show furniture. Just because it's relevant, doesn't mean you can't do better.

The Mercedes logo isn't a car. The Virgin Atlantic logo isn't an airplane. The Apple logo isn't a computer..."


As a newbie, this really makes sense and became an eye-opener to me and I think a guiding star on how I can explore and craft with creativity and elegance in my next projects.
