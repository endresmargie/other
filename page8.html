Paintings and Other Artworks

“Every artist was once an ameteur”
― Ralph Waldo Emerson

As a start-up artist-wanna be, I think these are my work as an amateur painter. I started painting when I was still working in the corporate world because apparently, I can't afford the art materials when I was still a student. I remembered how happy I was when they got delivered at my front door because finally, I would be able to experience how to paint using acrylic on canvas which I wanted to do ever since.


My favorite techniques are pouring and abstract because I get to explore. It allows you boundless, surprising, and an extraordinary result which usually left me in awe. After all, 'there is no must in art because art if free."― Wassily Kandinsky
