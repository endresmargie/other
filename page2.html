About Me

So here's my story...

"Always come from a place of help." The most common and my favorite line from my mentors and I guess my guiding star as a freelancer  when I decided to establish ME Freelancing and Virtual Services as a business.

Crafting has been in my interest since then. It started in grade school when my teachers give us art activities. I get excited and enjoy every time I'm holding art materials ready to create something new and beautiful.

When I saw the opportunity where I can do both my passion and helping other businesses, I did not hesitate and immediately grab the course offered by Filipino Virtual Assistance and learn from one of the best mentors in the Philippines.

Reliability, attitude, talent, and experience are the things that I can offer to my future and clients and associates as we work together.I am excited to work with you and would love to see your business succeed with our collaborative and strategic effort.
